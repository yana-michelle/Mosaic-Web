ANCHORED KIDS
Anchored Kids is our Sunday morning ministry designed for kids ages Preschool through 5th grade.
We offer Anchored Kids at our 9:00 am (Esapñol), 10:30 am & 12:00 pm (English) worship experiences.
Each week, our amazing kids staff ensures that our kids environments are designed with your kids in mind.
We keep it—Safe, On their level, Fun, and All about family!
